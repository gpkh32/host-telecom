## Why the Czech Republic, of All Places?

Good question. It turns out the Czech Republic is quietly becoming Central Europe's digital hub. Geographically it sits right in the middle of Europe — great latency to Germany, Austria, Poland, the UK, you name it. EU data regulations apply, so your data stays in Europe. And compared to Frankfurt or Amsterdam, prices tend to be more reasonable.

Host-Telecom has leaned hard into this positioning. Their Tier III-equivalent data center offers redundant power and cooling paths, meaning if one component needs maintenance, the lights don't go out. They've also received EU funding (OPPIK) for data center expansion — which isn't something a fly-by-night operation typically does.
